Information regarding your Scottrade brokerage account.
Dear Client,

The U.S. Postal Service (USPS) recently informed us of your new address. To be certain you don’t miss any account-related communication for your Scottrade®account, please complete the Contact Information Update form and return it to your local Scottrade®team or email the form to: support@scottrade.com. Additionally, you can update your information online by logging in to your account and following the path below:

My Account > My Information & Preferences > Contact Information

Previous mailings sent to your address on file may have been returned as undeliverable by the USPS. Accounts for which Scottrade receives mail returned as undeliverable are classified as “missing” to protect client information.

State laws require financial institutions to escheat assets of clients with whom they lose contact. If contact is not made, assets within your account may be subject to escheatment.

If you have any questions, please contact your local Scottrade®team. Please disregard this email if you've recently updated your information.

Sincerely,

Scottrade, Inc.
